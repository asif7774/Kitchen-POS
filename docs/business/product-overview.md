# Kitchen POS — Product Overview

## What is Kitchen POS?

Kitchen POS is an offline-first point-of-sale system built specifically for Indian restaurants. It runs directly on your Windows or macOS computer — no internet connection required, no monthly subscription, and no data sent to external servers. Everything stays on your hardware.

The system handles the full restaurant workflow: taking orders, sending tickets to the kitchen, printing GST-compliant bills, tracking inventory, managing staff, and generating reports — all from a single terminal.

---

## Who is it for?

Kitchen POS is designed for small to mid-size restaurants operating from a single location with one billing terminal. It suits:

- Standalone restaurants, cafés, and dhabas
- Cloud kitchens with a single billing point
- Food stalls and quick-service outlets
- Any GST-registered or unregistered food business in India

---

## Key Benefits

- **Works without internet** — power cuts, network outages, and SIM failures do not affect operations
- **GST-compliant invoicing** — bills meet all legal requirements: sequential invoice numbers, HSN codes, CGST and SGST line items
- **No recurring fees** — pay once, use forever; no SaaS subscription
- **Your data stays with you** — no cloud account, no third-party access to your sales data
- **Thermal printer support** — direct USB connection, no drivers needed
- **Fast and simple** — designed for touchscreen use; staff learn in minutes

## First-Time Setup

When you open Kitchen POS for the first time, complete these six steps before going live:

1. **Log in** with the default admin PIN: `1234`
2. **Go to Settings** and enter your outlet name, address, and GSTIN
3. **Go to Settings → Printer** and click **Test Print** to confirm your thermal printer works
4. **Go to Menu** and add your categories and items — set the correct HSN code and GST rate for each item
5. **Go to Tables** and set up your floor plan with your table names and sections
6. **Change the admin PIN immediately** — Settings → Staff → Edit. The default PIN `1234` must not be used in a live restaurant.

> After setup, take your first backup: Settings → Backup → Export Backup. Store it on a USB drive or external storage.
